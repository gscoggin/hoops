## About

**Hoops.io** is a browser-based 1v1 basketball game built as a second-grade school **invention project**. The goal was simple: invent something fun that people can play together. The result is a two-player arcade basketball game set inside Madison Square Garden, featuring ten of the greatest NBA players of all time.

No downloads. No installs. Just open the link and play.

---

## How to Play

Two players share one keyboard. Pick your favorite NBA legend, then go head-to-head in a 2-minute game. Most points wins.

### Controls

| Action | Player 1 | Player 2 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Move | `W A S D` | `↑ ← ↓ →` |
| Jump | `W` | `↑` |
| Shoot | Hold `SPACE`, release to fire | Hold `ENTER`, release to fire |
| Block | `F` | `Right Shift` |

**Shooting tip:** Hold the shoot button longer to shoot from farther away. A quick tap is a layup — you need to be close to the basket. The longer you hold, the more power you build for a mid-range or long-range shot.

### Tip-Off

Every game starts with a **3-second countdown** followed by a **jump ball** at center court. Race to the middle and jump to grab the tip! After each basket, the ball returns to center for another jump ball.
